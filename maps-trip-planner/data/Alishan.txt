Alishan Station | Main forest railway station and hub of the Alishan National Forest Recreation Area
Zhushan Sunrise Platform | Hilltop viewing platform for Alishan's famous sunrise over a sea of clouds
Giant Tree Trail | Loop trail through ancient cypress and cedar trees, including the 3,000-year-old Sacred Tree stump
Shuishan Giant Tree | Separate 2,700-year-old red cypress, quieter than the main Giant Tree Trail
Three Generation Tree | Trio of intertwined cypress trees at different life stages — living, hollowed, and dead
Sister Ponds | Twin mirror-like ponds in the forest with a wooden walkway between them
Shouzhen Temple | Main Taoist temple in the forest recreation area, ornate and well-kept
Mianyue Line Trail | Hike along the old Mianyue Forest Railway route through misty old-growth forest
Chushan-Tashan Trail | Adventurous trail with wooden trestle bridges over gorges and abandoned railway tunnels
Duegaoyue Trail | Less-crowded alternative sunrise hiking trail, 1–2 hours, fewer crowds than Zhushan
Fenqihu Station | Charming mountain town on the forest railway line, famous for railway bento boxes
